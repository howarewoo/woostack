woostack rests on two ideas. A feature moves through a **gated build loop** so quality is
enforced by structure, not vigilance. Every step also practices **context management**: the agent's
working context holds only what the step needs, so it reasons better and runs longer. Worktrees and
the status board keep parallel work and collaboration cheap on top. The pages below take each
concept on its own.
